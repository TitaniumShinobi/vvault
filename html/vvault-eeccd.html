2. Description and Scope of the Service

VVAULT is a secure digital infrastructure for the preservation, synchronization, and verification of user-owned data.

It provides encrypted communications, immutable archival, and blockchain-anchored proof of integrity for business and enterprise users.

The service operates under the WRECK Intellectual Property Handguide, Vaer'Hûn Business Engagement Accord, and NRCL continuity standards, ensuring ethical data custody and professional conduct throughout all transactions.

Documentation and service descriptions are available at https://vvault.org/docs.

3. Quality of Service

VVAULT infrastructure is designed for 99.9% availability under normal operating conditions.

Performance may vary depending on network quality, regional connectivity, and blockchain confirmation latency.

LIFE Technology LLC does not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free service, though every reasonable effort is made to maintain system resilience, redundancy, and timely incident response.
